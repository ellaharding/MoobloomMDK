
Moobloom Mod
-------------------------------------------
Custom mod to fully implement the Moobloom into Minecraft
==============================
Features-
    - Naturally generates Mooblooms who will roam their surroundings
    - Mooblooms can be manually spawned through crafting spawn egg (allowed in survival!)
    - Recipe for spawn egg is the below format,
      where F is any variety of flower, D is a diamond,L is leather and B is beef.
        FFF
        FDF
        LBL
    - Mooblooms can be bred together through using daffodils,
      and will follow the player when the have a daffodil in their hand.
